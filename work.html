I am a Product Analyst at Huron Consulting Group. I

  • Provide at the elbow, peer-to-peer support to physicians, nurses, and other healthcare providers throughout their organization's Go-Live Epic implementation.
  • Perform workflow analysis while collaborating with healthcare providers and administration to maximize efficiency and efficacy for the end users.
  • Communicate directly with Epic support staff and system analysts/builders to triage system issues for resolution priority
  • Utilize my unique background to facilitate communication between healthcare providers and Epic implementation staff/hospital administration. I have practical clinical experience as a healthcare provider from my time as a medical student at the University of Minnesota and I will be finishing my Bachelor of Science degree in Computer Science from Oregon State University in December 2017. This combination of clinical and technical expertise gives me a unique perspective on electronic health record systems and allows to me to understand the perspectives of both the healthcare providers and the Epic system analysts, builders, and administrators.
