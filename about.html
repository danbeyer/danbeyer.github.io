About Me

I have always been fascinated with computers, even from a very early age. Some of my fondest memories growing up were of my brother, myself, and my dad sitting around our old MS DOS PC and trying out new shareware games and programs. I always figured I would end up with a career in technology focusing on computer science. These plans took a back seat while I attended the University of Minnesota Medical School where I gained valuable clinical experience and a deep understanding of our nation's healthcare system (the good and the bad). I am now a Product Analyst at Huron Consulting Group in the healthcare division where I develop and implement our proprietary revenue cycle software for our client healthcare organizations.
